Dive buddies
Summary
Divebuddies is a comprehensive diving companion app tailored for scuba divers,
snorkelers, and underwater explorers. It enables users to discover dive sites, plan trips,
log dives, and connect with a global diving community — making it ideal for both
beginners and seasoned divers.

Trip Planning & Expense Management: Plan dives, dive logs, and manage related
expenses.
Dive Site Explorer: Find global dive locations with data like depth, visibility, marine
life & reviews.
Community & Social: Join diver communities, post experiences, and engage in
discussions.
Dive Logs: Track and document dive details and performance.
Marine Life Guide: Learn about ocean species and conservation.
AI Recommendations: Get dive site suggestions based on profile & activity.
Content Moderation: AI filters out offensive content for a safer space.
